Everything you need to know about hybrid teams

Our AI engineers are specialized AI models trained on millions of coding patterns. They write, test, and review code just like human engineers, but faster and continuously. Each AI engineer is powered by leading models like Claude Opus, GPT-5, or Gemini, configured specifically for their role.

One Work Unit equals roughly one discrete task - an API endpoint, a UI component, a bug fix, or a test suite. It's output-based, not time-based. Think of it as "digital hours" - a measure of productive output. 1 human hour typically produces about 10 Work Units worth of output.
